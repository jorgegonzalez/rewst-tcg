## Da Rules

- Each player starts with 0 features, and 0 customers.
- To gain features, players need to deploy to production.
- To gain customers, players need to play specific cards or activate certain abilities.
- The first player to reach 30 features, or 10 customers, wins.


### Deploying to Production

- To deploy to production, tap any number of creatures you control. For each creature tapped, you deploy features equal to that creature's toughness.


### Challenges

- On your turn, you may tap a creature you control to challenge a tapped creature an opponent controls. You compare each card's power and toughness, and determine the winner. The losing creature is sent to its controller's graveyard.
